What is Gelcoat?

Gelcoat is a gel-like composition used for creation of decorative and protective coating of composite products. As a rule, gelcoat is a polyester resin with the addition of dye. The gelcoat is applied as the first layer on the matrix and after it has hardened, the reinforcing layers (glass fabrics or carbon fabrics) are applied over it and impregnated with the binder (polyester resin). Thus, after removing the product from the matrix, the front surface of the product is covered with gelcoat. Specialized gelcoats can also give the product\'s surface resistance to mechanical impact and aggressive environments.
